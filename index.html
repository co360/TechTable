Who are we

About Us

Tech Table is an initiative by IITM students to provide one platform for the Engineers "From Learning to Earning". We aim to train Engineers and give them practical Experience to use Niche Technology through our "Start-up Connect" module, where we connect engineers to Tech Start-up.
We also provide a platform for the freelancer to earn money by their skills. we have expertise in a different field to provide technical support to early-stage startup to grow rapidly in their field.
